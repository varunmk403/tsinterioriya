DIOR


Christian Dior is know as Designer Of Dreams, which is the inspiration for the concept that is dreamy and luxurious. Dior is all about elegant and luxurious. The retail store had to look luxurious and different when a person enters the store. The concept is inspired by dreamy, that’s why the colour scheme is white with hint of gold and black. Most of the areas is kept white colour so it can highlight the product.

MARC JACOBS

This kiosk is for Marc Jacobs. It is located in fashion week. The kiosk is about the daisy perfume collection and the makeup products. The kiosk is covered with real daisy flower. There is daisy in the kiosk because the perfume is all about the daisy. The kiosk is pastel colours and a hint of black as all the makeup products are black in colour.

SULA VINEYARDS

It is an Indian brand which makes wines on our Indian land in Nashik. Key drivers for the design of this fit out were inspired by the name itself “ Sula Vineyards”. The concept it “ RECREATING THE EXPERIENCE OF VINEYARDS AND WINE TOUR”. The design incorporates rustic features that make references to the lifestyle on the vineyards both past & present, with highly customized furniture for this design. Weaved throughout is a sense of sula wine – unlike stepping into a typical office space, staff and visitors find themselves in more of a welcoming family home with an open environment.
